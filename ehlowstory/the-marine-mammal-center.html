What They Do

The Marine Mammal Center (TMMC) has locations mainly along the California coasts. They find/receive calls for marine mammals in need of help, rescue them, diagnose them, treat them, and release them!



Stats

TMMC is known for many incredible feats, including but not limited to:

  • Being the largest marine mammal center in America
  • Being around for 50 years
  • Rescuing 24000 marine mammals
  • Having a 99% success rate (returning patients to the ocean)
