About This Website
Why? What Can You Do?

Why?

This website was created to spread awareness about occurences like Ehlow getting stranded. People knowing about these things will hopefully try to not cause them, or even prevent them. (I suppose that TMMC also likes volunteers.)



What Can You Do?

What can you do? It's fairly simple. The easiest way is to not interact with animals in their habitats, like Ehlow. You could also try to protect their habitats, through methods such as reducing pollution, building, and fishing. And, of course, you can spread this knowledge with everyone you can.
